What is this?

This is a tool for Eve Online aimed at being able to quickly price check items in bulk.

How are prices calculated?

Prices calculated from recent market data from Eve Online's ESI API. Items with low (or no) market volume use CCP's pricing estimates.

What does a purple row mean?

Purple rows are items that {{.UI.SiteName}} had a hard time finding an accurate and current price for. This is usually caused by low (or no) market volume for that item.

Caveats/Known Issues

  • There's no reliable way to get prices of blueprint copies (BPCs). Due to this, {{.UI.SiteName}} doesn't even try to guess a price for them.
  • Non-english formats don't always work properly.

Is there an API?

Yes! A very simple one, but it will probably get you what you want. Click here to see the API Documentation.
